About the FFA Organization

The National FFA Organization prepares members for leadership and career success through agricultural education. Founded in 1928, FFA today serves hundreds of thousands of students across all 50 states, including right here in Claude, Texas.

FFA helps students develop public speaking, teamwork, and technical agricultural skills. Through classroom learning, supervised agricultural experiences (SAEs), and leadership opportunities, members build confidence and connections that last a lifetime.

Claude FFA continues this proud tradition by encouraging students to grow as leaders, serve their community, and pursue careers in the agriculture industry.

Mission Statement

The FFA mission is to make a positive difference in the lives of students by developing their potential for premier leadership, personal growth, and career success through agricultural education.

FFA at Claude High School

Students in Claude FFA can participate in a wide range of activities such as livestock showing, agricultural mechanics, speaking events, and leadership contests. Members also engage in community service projects and take part in local, district, and state conventions.
