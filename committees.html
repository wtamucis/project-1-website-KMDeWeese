Chapter Committees

Claude FFA organizes its Program of Activities through three main divisions: Growing Leaders, Building Communities, and Strengthening Agriculture. Students can be assigned or can sign up to help with the projects in each division.

Holden Tice & Grady Denson Ag. Mech. Project 2024-'25

Growing Leaders

This division focuses on activities that help members develop leadership skills, confidence, and scholarship.

  • Greenhand orientation / degree work
  • Chapter Meeting Programs
  • Officer Retreat or Training

Building Communities

This division includes community service and activities that promote FFA in Claude and Armstrong County.

  • FFA petting zoo / Elementary Ag Day
  • Community Clean-up or Service Projects
  • Banquet Planning and Set Up

Strengthening Agriculture

This division supports agricultural literacy, contest teams, and recruitment into the ag program.

  • CDE/LDE Practice & Coaching
  • Ag Advocacy or Classroom Presentations
  • Ag Mechanics, Skills, and Livestock
